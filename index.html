About Fikir

I am a contemporary synesthete artist whose work explores the delicate balance between chaos and harmony. Through vibrant colors and dynamic compositions, each piece tells a story of human emotion and experience.

With over a decade of artistic exploration, My work spans multiple mediums including traditional paintings, digital art, and mixed media installations. The journey began with a fascination for how colors can evoke memories and emotions through synesthetic experiences.

Living with Synesthesia

Where colors have sounds and emotions have textures

Synesthetic Paintings of Songs

Synesthesia is a neurological condition where stimulation of one sensory pathway leads to automatic, involuntary experiences in a second sensory pathway. For me, colors produce sounds, numbers have personalities, and emotions manifest as vivid textures and shapes.

Each painting below was created while listening to a specific song—the music shaped the colors and strokes you see.
